Lilavatibai Podar High School

The Lilavatibai Podar High School is aimed to provide an enriching educational environment where students can excel in academics, be responsible citizens, and become successful future leaders in our global society.

1921 - 1930

The Anandilal Podar Trust (erstwhile, The Anandilal Podar Charitable Society / Anandilal Education Society) was founded in 1921 with a donation by Mr Anandilal Podar of Rs. 2,01,000/- to Mahatma Gandhi, for the 'Tilak Swaraj Fund'. The Educational Complex was founded under the inspiration of Late Shri Pithashree Anandilal Podar in 1927 as a small exclusive school, for all those who love their children and want to impart quality education. In 1930, Mahatma Gandhi and Mr. Jamnalal Bajaj, owing to their political pre-occupation, retired from the Trusteeship of the Society, and Mr. Madan Mohan Malviya became the President of the Society, till his demise in 1946. Thereafter, the Society was guided by Raja Ramdeo Anandilal Podar.

1987

Lilavatibai Podar High School, is a co-educational English medium, unaided institution founded in 1987 by Late Shri Ganesh Narayan Podar. The School stirves to impart sound, liberal education aimed at the development of the total personality of the child.

2014

Since its inception, The school has reached great heights with the collective effort of the management, heads and staff. It has been successful in implementing its vision of creating global citizens year after year.

The only thing constant in life is ‘Change’. Our educational existence since 1987 and its historic legacy, has been an incredible journey in nurturing academic talent and hone life’s skills in every student for a life ahead of their schooling years. At LPHS we take responsibility to encourage our students to dream big by reminding them to be risk takers and never limit themselves because of other’s limited imagination. Our endevour has always been to work in partnership that will positively impact our student’s academic, social and emotional development helping us to Educate, Enlighten and Empower the students.

At LPHS we strive to foster ‘lifelong learning’ and create independent learners and responsible citizens. It is through our academic and co-curricular activities that we entwine the understanding of human relations, respect for fellow beings and service to the community.

To prepare our students for an ever changing world, we train them to adopt the right soft skills, this is a part of their everyday learning. Planned, age appropriate workshops are conducted regularly, Topics on Gender sensitivity, Internet etiquette and responsible use of social media, Sensitivity and service to the community, Self-study habits are addressed.

To influence and impact our students learning, it is a culture at LPHS to invite our alumni to speak to our students of their experiences from their varied professional backgrounds.

The school’s results have always proved to be the best year after year, both in the ICSE and ISC board exams. The holistic learning at LPHS has enabled our students to secure top positions in universities in India and abroad, carving a niche for themselves and placed LPHS on the world map.

LPHS is among the first ICT enabled schools in the country and have progressed to be the first again to adopt the use of Mobile phones in our teaching learning process.
